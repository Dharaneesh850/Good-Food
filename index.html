Our Recipe

The spicy curries of the South to the creamy gravies of the North, Indian cuisine is known for its generous use of spices like turmeric cumin coriander cardamom, and garam masala. rice wheat and lentils form the backbone of indian meals accompanied by vegetables meats pickles, and chutneys. south indian cuisine is famous for dosa idli sambarand coconut based dishes, while North India is known for naan paneer butter masala biryani and kebabs. Eastern states bring in dishes like fish curry and pakhala, and the West showcases flavors through dhokla pav bhaji and thepla. street food is an essential part of the experience with chaat samosas pani puri and vada pav being favorites across generations
